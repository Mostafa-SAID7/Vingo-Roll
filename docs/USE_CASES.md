## 📖 User Stories

### Shopping & Browsing

#### Story 1: Discovering Products
**As a** homeowner  
**I want to** browse curtain categories  
**So that** I can find the right style for my room

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- ✅ View all product categories
- ✅ See product images and prices
- ✅ Filter by category
- ✅ Sort by price or rating

**User Flow:**
1. Visit homepage
2. Click "Shop Now" or navigate to Shop
3. Browse product grid
4. Apply filters (category, price, rating)
5. Sort results
6. Click product for details

---

#### Story 2: Searching for Specific Products

## 🎭 Edge Cases

### Case 1: Out of Stock Product
**Scenario:** Customer wants product that's out of stock  
**Solution:** 
- Show "Out of Stock" status
- Offer "Notify When Available" option
- Suggest similar products

### Case 2: Custom Size Not Listed
**Scenario:** Customer needs non-standard size  
**Solution:**
- Provide custom order form
- Offer measuring service
- Give quote for custom size

### Case 3: Color Uncertainty
**Scenario:** Customer unsure about color choice  
**Solution:**
- Offer fabric samples ($5 each)
- Provide color matching service
- Show room visualization

### Case 4: Installation Concerns
**Scenario:** Customer worried about installation  
**Solution:**
- Provide installation guide
- Offer professional installation
- Include video tutorials

## 📈 Success Metrics

### Key Performance Indicators

**Conversion Rate:**
- Target: 2-3% of visitors make purchase
- Measure: Completed orders / Total visitors

**Average Order Value:**
- Target: $250-$350
- Measure: Total revenue / Number of orders

**Customer Satisfaction:**
- Target: 4.5+ star rating
- Measure: Average review rating

**Return Rate:**
- Target: < 5%
- Measure: Returns / Total orders

**Time to Purchase:**
- Target: < 10 minutes
- Measure: First visit to checkout completion
